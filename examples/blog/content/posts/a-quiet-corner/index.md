Not everything has to be a stage. Some places are just rooms — small, lit, and lived in. This site is one of those.

I do not post here to perform. I post to think out loud in a corner that belongs to me, where the lighting does not change to keep me scrolling and no stranger's anger arrives uninvited.

The big web will always be loud. I do not want to fix it or fight it. I just want a quiet corner of my own to come back to, and to leave the door unlocked for anyone who wanders by.
