**Shipping & returns.** Items are made to order and ship within 7–10 days.
Misprints or damage in transit are replaced free — email within 14 days.

**Privacy.** This site sets no cookies and runs no analytics. Payment and shipping
details are handled by the external checkout provider, not collected here.

**特定商取引法に基づく表記.** A shop selling to customers in Japan would put its
特定商取引法 notice here (seller name, address, contact, prices, delivery and
return terms). crofty makes this a normal fixed page — `content/legal.md`.
