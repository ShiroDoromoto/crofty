This is a demonstration site built with **crofty**. The author below is an
example — a stand-in for the person whose blog this would be.

Everything here is plain Markdown rendered to a static site on a domain its
owner controls. No platform sits in the middle, nothing phones home, and the
whole thing can be carried off to ordinary Hugo at any time. The posts are just
files in a folder; this page is one of them.

If you are reading this because you are deciding whether to start your own small
site: you do not need permission, and you do not need much. A folder of text and
a place to put it is enough.
