Everything on this page is written in Markdown. Here is a quick tour of what
that gives you.

## Headings keep the structure clear

Use them to break a longer piece into sections a reader can scan.

You get **bold**, *italics*, and `inline code` without thinking about markup.

- Lists are just dashes
- One item per line
- Nested points work too

> Block quotes set apart a line worth pausing on.

For code, fence it:

```
crofty build
crofty deploy
```

And [links](https://crofty.blog) are plain text in brackets. That is the whole
toolkit — no editor, no platform, just text you can read in any file.
